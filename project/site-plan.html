Overview

Purpose

Enhance the quality of pet care and improve the relationships between dogs and their owners in the city. The website will serve as a directory for professional dog trainers in Ecuador, allowing trainers to create profiles showcasing their expertise, services, and customer reviews.

Audience

The audience for the website includes dog owners, trainers, and pet enthusiasts. Dog owners range from first-time pet parents seeking basic training and socialization guidance to experienced owners needing specialized services like advanced obedience or behavior correction. Busy owners who prioritize convenience will also find value in the platform. Additionally, pet enthusiasts interested in improving their bond with their dogs and learning more about responsible ownership will benefit from the siteâ€™s resources and connections to trusted trainers.
